By experimenting with different perspectives of motor imagery perspective on different levels of muscles distances relative to the descending spinal pathway, we identified different sensitivity levels. Our observation that third-person visual imagery can match kinesthetic imagery in enhancing corticospinal excitability highlights its potential as a practical and effective strategy for training and neurorehabilitation.
